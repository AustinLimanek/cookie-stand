Our Mission:

We have been making our deliciously irresistible Salmon Cookies for over ten years. Originally out of the Pacific Northwest, we have now expanded to four continents! However, we still have the classic subtle fishy undertones in each crunchy bite of every cookie we make from scratch. You will also enjoy the chocolate, coconut, and sea salt chunks that bring that fishy goodness to the next level. Our cream cheese frosting adds a smooth creaminess that makes it difficult to have less than four Salmon Cookies in one sitting. Visit a store near you the next time you need a sweet and savory treat.
